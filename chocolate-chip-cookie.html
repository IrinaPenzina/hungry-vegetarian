Chocolate Chip Cookie

This is a classic Nestlé Toll House's Original Chocolate Chip Cookie recipe. The cookies turn out amazing, and it's very easy to make. It took me some time to try different recipes, but this one is my all-time favorite.

Share recipe

Ingredients:

2 1/4 cups of all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon of baking soda
1 teaspoon of salt
1 cup (2 sticks) of butter, softened
3/4 cup of sugar
3/4 cup of packed brown sugar
1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
2 large eggs
2 cups (12-oz. pkg.) of chocolate chips
1 cup chopped nuts (if omitting, add 1-2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour)

In a bowl, beat butter, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until creamy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.

In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt. Beat in flour mixture gradually in your dough. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts.

Preheat the oven to 375 F. Scoop dough with a teaspoon and drop it onto an ungreased baking sheet. Bake for 9 - 11 minutes until golden brown. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes.
