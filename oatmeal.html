Oatmeal with Coconut Milk

For this absolutely delicious breakfast, all you need is 1-minute oatmeal, Original coconut milk, and some fruit, berries, nuts, or whatever you prefer. No need to cook anything. Just pour the dry oats into a bowl and pour coconut milk right from the fridge. Cut up some fruits and berries and let it sit for 3-5 minutes. ENJOY!
