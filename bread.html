Bread

It's easy to make bread, and it doesn't require any special equipment. All you need is four ingredients and a couple of hours of your time. If you follow this recipe, you will get delicious bread with a crunchy crust. My family absolutely loves it.

Share recipe

Ingredients:

3 cups of all-purpose flour, or bread flour
1/2 tbsp of kosher salt
2 1/4 tsp of instant or active dry yeast packet
1 1/2 cups of warm water, 105 F

Start by combining all dry ingredients in a big bowl: flour, salt, and yeast. Next, bring the water to a temperature around 105 F and pour it into the bowl. Mix all the ingredients.

Wet your hands to knead the dough. Spin the bowl and squeeze it. Wet your hands again if the dough starts sticking to your hands.
Once you have finished, cover the dough with a towel or a napkin and let it rest for 30 minutes to an hour.

When the time is up, wet your hands again and grab the dough from one side, stretch it as far as you can, and fold it over. Do this 15 to 20 times. You want to make a tough ball out of it. Cover the bowl again and let it sit for another 30 minutes to one hour.

When the timer goes off, preheat your oven to 500F with your loaf baker inside. Spread some flour on the board and put the dough on it. Fold the edges of the dough. Turn it around and lift it and throw it several times.

When the the oven reaches 500F degrees pull the loaf baker out. The loaf baker will be hot so be careful. You can put flower in the baker and carefully lay your dough, seams down, in the baker. Alternatively, put some parchment paper down, spread some flour, and place the dough seams down on the parchment paper. Lay the paper and dough inside. Score the top of your dough with the scissors and close the lid. Put your loaf baker in the oven at 500F for 18 minutes. After 18 minutes, remove the lid and lower the temperature to 485 degrees. Bake the bread for another 25 to 35 minutes.

This is absolutely delicious bread with a crunchy crust.
