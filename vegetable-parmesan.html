Vegetable Parmesan

100% try this recipe! Even though it might take some time to make it. Especially if you don't have a big grill. However, it's absolutely worth it. The vegetables, sauce, and cheese create a perfect taste in your mouth. So good!

Share recipe

Ingredients:
1 medium eggplant
2 medium fennel bulbs
2 zucchini (green and yellow)
8 garlic cloves
1 (26-ounce) jar marinara sauce
3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
1 cup grated Parmesan
1 cup plain bread crumbs
Olive oil
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

I usually start with cutting the eggplant into 1/4 to 1/2-inch thick slices. Put the slices on the paper towel, and cover them with it. It has to suck out all the moisture out of the eggplant slices.

Next, trim and cut zucchini and fennel into 1/4-inch thick slices. Press garlic with a knife or shred it.
Grease up all the sliced vegetables with olive oil, salt and pepper them.

Grill your vegetables to soften them. I don't think that the way you grill them matters. Whatever way you have available, use it.

It's time for layers: sauce, eggplant, sauce, basil, mozzarella, Parmesan, fennel, sauce, basil, mozzarella, Parmesan, green zucchini, sauce, basil, mozzarella, Parmesan, yellow zucchini, sauce, basil, mozzarella, Parmesan, and finally bread crumbs and olive oil on top.
Pre-heat an oven to 350F. Put Vegetable Parmesan into the oven for 25 minutes.

Here it is a perfect meal in every sense. It goes really well with bread.
