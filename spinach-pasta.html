Creamy Spinach Pasta

Ingredients for Creamy Spinach Pasta are usually sitting in your fridge. Surprisingly when you put all of them together in one dish it turns out really good. I'm working on not having the second plate. Well, it did not work with this pasta. Yum!

Share recipe

Ingredients:
400g (14 oz) Pasta
1 big Onion
400g (14 oz) Mushrooms
300g (13 oz) Spinach
5 or more cloves of garlic
355ml (1.5 cup) Vegetable broth
240ml (1 cup) Cream Cheese
Parmesan Cheese
Salt
Pepper
Pepper Chili Flakes
Olive Oil

It's not a must-do, but it’s what I like to do - start making noodles first. I boil them, salt, drain as they are cooked. Only cook them to al dente or a bit less. They will cook more later. Pour some olive oil on top and close the lid. Meanwhile, the rest is cooking.

You need to chop up your onion. Warm up a pan and pour in some olive oil. Fry onions until they turn brown. Put them on a plate off to the side.

Now, do the same thing with your mushrooms in the same pan: chop them up, fry them in the pan until brown and remove them. Put them on the plate with your onions off to the side. (Note: depending on how you like your mushrooms and onions cooked, you can fry these together.)

Throw the spinach into the same pan and fry it until it is wilted. Add some salt and pepper. Remove spinach from the pan and drain the excess water.

Mince garlic and warm up some oil in the same pan. Fry garlic until brown, just a few minutes. Add vegetable broth, cream cheese, parmesan cheese, and pepper chili flakes. Melt cream cheese and stir the sauce for 3-4 minutes.

Now all you need to do is mix everything in the pan. First, dump all the veggies and stir them. Next, add pasta and stir it some more. Add some extra parmesan cheese for extra deliciousness.

Enjoy your absolutely delicious Creamy Spinach Pasta ❤️
