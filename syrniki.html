Syrniki - Cheese Pancakes

If you have cottage cheese sitting in your fridge, this recipe is 100% for you. It's super easy to make. My husband doesn't like cottage cheese, but he loves syrniki.

Share recipe

Ingredients:

2 cups of Cottage Cheese
1 Egg
1 tablespoon of Sugar
(About) 1,5 cups of Flour
Vegetable Oil
Add to your plate: sour cream and fruit

Let's begin! Put your cottage cheese into a bowl and break an egg. Mix them together. Next, add sugar and mix it again. Begin to add the flour. Add in a little at a time, mixing it until the dough is thick and can hold a shape.

Heat some vegetable oil and fry your cheesy pancakes until they turn brown.

I like to have them with sour cream and sweet berries and fruit. You can put some jam on top.
Enjoy your delicious breakfast!
