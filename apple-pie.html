Apple Pie

Apple pie is one of the best autumn treats with no tricks. During the apple season, I make two or three of them; you know what - the pie disappears in a couple of days every time. It is a great classic recipe. Make sure to check out the crust recipe too.

Share recipe

Ingredients:

find Pie Crust recipe here

6 medium thinly sliced apples
3/4 cup of white sugar
2 tbsp of all-purpose flour
3/4 tsp of ground cinnamon or pumpkin spice
1/4 tsp of salt
1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
1 tbsp of lemon juice

Put all the ingredients in the big bowl and mix them.

Split your crust dough into two pieces and roll them out separately. Spread apple filling evenly over one piece of dough on the baking pan. Cover the pie with the second piece. Make holes on the top.
Preheat the oven to 425 F. Bake the pie for 40 - 45 minutes.

Apple pie is ready. Enjoy it.
