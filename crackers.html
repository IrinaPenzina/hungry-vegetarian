Crackers

Crackers lovers who care about what they eat will love this recipe. Have you ever read the content on any crackers box? Those ingredients sound like they are out of a chemistry kit. Here's your healthy crackers recipe.

Share recipe

Ingredients:

1/2 cup of spent grain flour
1 1/2 cups of shredded cheese
2 tbsp of butter
1/2 cup of flour
1/3 cup of water
1/4 teaspoon of salt

Spent grain flour is not a mandatory ingredient. You can use regular flour instead of this one. My husband brews beer, and he grinds the grains into flour. We tried to add it to different bread doughs, but for now, we had success only with these crackers.
It actually gives this brown colour to crackers.

Mix all the ingredients (except the water) in the food processor or with a mixer.

You will get crumbs looking like this. Mix in the water now. Make sure that the dough is not too sticky. You may need to add more flour if this is the case. Cut it into two equal pieces.

With a rolling pin, roll out each piece of dough very thinly on parchment paper. Cut it into squares. Each piece of dough should be transferred to the baking sheet with parchment paper. The oven should be preheated to 450 F. Salt the top before putting it in the oven for 20 minutes.

It's perfect for your quickly improvised charcuterie board or even for a sophisticated one.
