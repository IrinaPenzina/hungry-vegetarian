Cookie Cake (no baking)

Super easy to make, no need to bake! ...But you will have to wait over a night to eat it. All you need are just three ingredients plus one more - patience.

Share recipe

Ingredients:

1 pack of cookies from a store. Try to find some that aren’t too sweet. I used belVita Breakfast Biscuits.
12 oz of Sour Cream
4 oz of Strawberry Jam – (or choose the flavor you prefer)

I used belVita Breakfast Biscuits. You can use any other cookies. Just make sure they aren’t too sweet.
Start by laying out your cookies on the plate or a tray where you want to make your cake.

Next, spread sour cream on top of the cookies. I put a layer of approximately a 1/2 inch of Sour Cream, but the more - the merrier.

On top of the sour cream spread some jam. Make sure there are no huge lumps of it.

Finally, close your cake with another layer of cookies. Cover it with a lid and put it in the fridge for the night.

Here it is! Ready and delicious. In the first picture, you see what the cake looks like after sitting in the fridge for one night. The second picture is the cake after two nights. It's super simple. Try it yourself.
