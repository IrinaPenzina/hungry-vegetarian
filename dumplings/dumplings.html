Vegetarian Dumplings

Vegetarian dumplings that we make with my husband. It's not just a meal cooking but also a great team building.

Share recipe

Dumpling dough recipe:

Wheat flour (w / s) - 0.5 kilograms (almost 4 faceted glasses);
Chicken eggs - 2 pieces;
Cold water - 200 grams (1 faceted glass);
Salt
