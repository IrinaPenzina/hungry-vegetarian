Pumpkin Pie

Here is a recipe for pumpkin pie made with real pumpkins. It is my favorite time of year to decorate my house with little pie pumpkins in autumn. It looks super cute, and they are waiting to be turned into a pie. To make the pie taste delicious, I use a variety of spices.

Share recipe

Ingredients:

find Pie Crust recipe here

2 large eggs
1 large egg yolk
1/2 cup of brown sugar
1/3 cup of white sugar
1/2 teaspoon of salt
2 teaspoons of pumpkin pie spice
1 teaspoon of ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon of ground cloves
1/4 teaspoon of cardamom
1/2 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest
1 cup of heavy cream
2 cups of homemade pumpkin purée or 1 pie pumpkin

I usually start with baking pumpkin. Cut a pumpkin into halves and scrape out the insides. Line a baking sheet or foil and place the pumpkin on top. Bake at 350F for about 1 hour to 1 hour and a half. You should be able to pierce them easily with a fork. Take them out of the oven and scoop out the pulp.

Find the crust recipe here if you want to make it from scratch. Next, make the filling. Beat the eggs in the large bowl. Mix brown sugar, white sugar, salt, pumpkin pie spice, ground ginger, ground nutmeg, ground cloves, cardamom, finely grated lemon zest, and heavy cream. Add pumpkin purée and mix it with a whisk.

Roll out the dough and lay it out on the baking pan. Spread pumpkin pie filling evenly over the dough.
Preheat the oven to 425 F. Bake the pie for 40 - 45 minutes.

Serve the pie with whip cream on top. The taste of autumn has never been better.
