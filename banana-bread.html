Banana Bread

Yes, banana bread is delicious, and I sometimes crave a piece. However, I usually make it because we got too many bananas, and I did eat them all on time. It doesn't matter why you love banana bread. It is a great recipe to try.

Share recipe

Ingredients:

1/3 cup of butter
1 cup of white sugar
2 eggs
4 ripe bananas
1 cup of sour cream
2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
2 1/2 cups of flour
Optional: walnuts, peanuts

Before adding to the bowl, soften the butter and mash the bananas. Cream butter with sugar very well in the bowl. Combine the eggs, bananas, sour cream, soda, and salt, and mix all the ingredients. Gradually add flour as you mix. The dough is going to look something like this.

Grease up a baking pan and pour the dough into it. If you want to add nuts, go ahead and do that. I added a bunch of walnuts and peanuts.

Preheat the oven to 325F and bake for 1 hour.

Look at this slice. The bread comes out very moist and delicious.
